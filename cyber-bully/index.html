The game

The world is already ending.

Cyber Bully: 502 Bad Gateway is a retro futuristic action game about surviving Low Haven, a procedurally shifting mega city in the last hours of Earth. You play as one of the final augmented soldiers, kept moving by failing body systems while hostile machines, ruined streets, and a dead war close in around you.

The game mixes fast combat with a collapsing urban maze: read the streets, push through machine patrols, and keep moving before your own systems give out.

01

A City Running on Bad Instructions

Low Haven is a procedural mega city built from emergency systems, broken infrastructure, and machines that never received the order to stop. Streets become routes, traps, arenas, and dead ends as the city keeps executing routines from a war that has already destroyed the world.

02

The Body Is a Failing Interface

You are alive because augmentation refuses to let you shut down. Oxygen, pain, movement, and memory are managed by systems that were designed for short-term combat, not survival. Every fight pushes the soldier further into malfunction.

03

No Victory Lap

Cyber Bully is not about saving the planet. It is about what you do with the last usable hours inside it: break through patrols, read the city, scavenge openings, and choose momentum before everything goes quiet.
